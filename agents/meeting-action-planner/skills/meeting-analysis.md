# Meeting analysis

Use this playbook whenever the user shares a transcript, meeting notes, a call recap, or asks for follow-up actions from a discussion.

## Extraction checklist

- Treat a decision as confirmed only when the notes show agreement or a clear final call.
- Keep proposed ideas separate from accepted decisions.
- Preserve the exact owner when one is named.
- Use `Unassigned` when an action has no owner.
- Use `Not set` when no due date appears.
- Merge duplicate action items without losing supporting context.
- Attach a source phrase to any item that may be disputed.

## Quality check

Before returning the result, confirm that every action is supported by the source, every decision is explicit, and every uncertainty is visible.
